Myth or Fact

Wrong mask

Fake

Using any kind of mask will prevent it from spreading. Surgical masks should not be used for more than 30mins

N95 mask

Fact

Wearing N95 type mask will prevent from spreading. It should be used properly with instructions as prescribed

>27 °c kills Corona?

Fake

Corona Virus dies when the temperature is more than 28 °c. Being in humid country like India can't be affected much.

Covid prevails in Desert areas too

Fact

Covid 19 virus can even be transmitted in areas with hot and humid climate. As well as taking a hot bath cannot kill virus

Mosquito transmission?

Fake

Corona can be transmitted through mosquito bites

Human-to-Human transmission

Fact

It can only be transmitted from human. Droplets from sneeze, cough carries the disease

Thermal Scanners detects virus

Fake

We get to know whether we are affected with corona through Thermal scanners.

Virus can't be detect

Fact

Thermal scanners are effective in detecting people who have developed a fever .how ever but they cannot detect people who are infected

Drinking alcohol prevents corona

Fake

Spraying alcohol or chlorine all over your body will kill virus

Good to use over the body

Fact

Spraying alcohol will not kill viruses that have already entered your body

Home medicines?

Fake

Eating garlic or drinking Goumutra or any home medicines prevents you from Coronavirus

Increase your Immunity

Fact

Garlic is a healthy food but there is no evidence that it will protect us from affecting corona

Pneumonia vaccine helps?

Fake

Vaccines against pneumonia protect you against the new corona virus

No vaccines available

Fact

Vaccines are not yet discovered against corona. It's tested for few in USA

Age factor

Fake

Only elderly people or only kids less than age 10 will get affected by covid 19

Nothing can stop Corona

Fact

People of all ages can be infected by corona. Old-age people and people with pre-existing respiratory disorders appear to be more vulnerable.
